Mr. Baus - Mr. Baus is the head of a design agency looking to hire a new intern. Specifically he's looking for a young designer with a deep sense of self. More so, he'd like to know more about this designer through a personal statement, rather than portfolio.

Alison Wonderland - Just opened up a restaurant and is looking for a hip young designer to design her logo. She has an extremely specific palette, loves to shuffle through online designer portfolios. After searching through so many portfolios online, she's getting exhausted and is looking for portfolios that get to the point and show her examples of work quickly.
